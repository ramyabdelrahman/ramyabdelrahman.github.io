Professional Experience

My professional experience connects legal research, legislative reform, civic education, policy analysis, project management, and public affairs.

Legal and Civic Background

I have professional experience in legal research, legislative reform, policy analysis, civic education, and civil society work. In Egypt, I worked as a lawyer and participated in projects related to constitutional amendments, legislative reform, parliamentary monitoring, and public awareness.

My professional background helped me understand how law, public institutions, and civic participation are connected. This experience also shaped my interest in using legal knowledge to support public service, institutional reform, and future work in business, corporate law, and public policy.

Egyptian Democratic Institute

I served as Executive Director of the Egyptian Democratic Institute from 2009 to 2015. In that role, I worked on civic education initiatives, democratic development projects, voter education campaigns, and reports related to public institutions and legal reform.

My work involved managing projects, preparing reports, coordinating with teams, and supporting public education programs. These responsibilities strengthened my leadership, communication, research, and project management skills.

Connection to My Future Career
