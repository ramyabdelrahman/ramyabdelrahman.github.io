Bachelor of Arts in History

I am a senior undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in History at George Mason University in the College of Humanities and Social Sciences. My expected graduation is Spring 2027.

My academic interests include American legal history, constitutional development, public policy, democratic institutions, legal research, and the relationship between law, government, and society.

My undergraduate studies are preparing me for law school and a future legal career in the United States. They also allow me to connect my previous legal education and professional experience with historical and institutional research.
