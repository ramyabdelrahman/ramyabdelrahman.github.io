Leadership and Civic Engagement

Executive Director

I served as Executive Director of the Egyptian Democratic Institute from July 2009 through June 2015. In this role, I was responsible for organizational leadership, program development, civic education, community outreach, and institutional planning.

My work focused on increasing public awareness of democratic participation, supporting community engagement, and developing educational activities connected to citizenship, governance, and public affairs.

I also coordinated projects, supervised administrative activities, communicated with partners, and helped organize training programs, workshops, and public events.

Organization: Egyptian Democratic Institute

Position: Executive Director

Period: July 2009–June 2015

Focus: Civic education, leadership, and community engagement

Policy and Democratic Development

Public Policy and Institutional Development

My professional experience included work related to public policy, democratic development, organizational management, and the relationship between government institutions and civil society.
