Personal Introduction

My name is Ramy Abdelrahman. I am a History student at George Mason University with a strong interest in law, public policy, civic education, democratic development, and community engagement.

My academic goal is to complete my bachelor’s degree in History and continue to law school. I am especially interested in the relationship between history, legal institutions, public policy, and the development of democratic societies.

Current University: George Mason University

Major: History

Academic Goal: Law School

Primary Interests: Law, public policy, civic education, and governance

Background and Professional Interests

Before continuing my education in the United States, I worked in civil society and civic engagement. My work included leadership, community outreach, public education, democratic participation, and institutional development.

I served as Executive Director of the Egyptian Democratic Institute from 2009 to 2015. In that position, I worked on civic education, democratic awareness, organizational leadership, and community-based activities.

My professional and academic experiences have shaped my interest in legal history, governance, democratic institutions, public policy, and the role of civic education in strengthening communities.

This portfolio brings together my academic background, professional experience, skills, and selected projects. It also demonstrates the web design techniques developed in my Web Authorship and Design course.
