Welcome to My Website

My name is Ramy Abdelrahman. I am a student at George Mason University studying history and preparing for law school. My academic interests include law, history, democracy, civic engagement, and public policy.

I earned an Associate of Arts and Sciences in General Studies from Germanna Community College, graduating summa cum laude. I continued my education at George Mason University to expand my knowledge of history, law, and public affairs.

My study of history helps me understand the development of legal institutions, political systems, and public policies. It also provides important context for examining contemporary legal and social issues.

My long-term goal is to attend law school and pursue a career that combines legal knowledge, historical understanding, and public service.

Academic and Professional Interests

I am particularly interested in democracy, civic education, human rights, public policy, and the rule of law. These subjects connect my professional experience with my current education and future career goals.

Before continuing my education in the United States, I worked in civil society and civic engagement. This experience strengthened my interest in public service and demonstrated the importance of informed participation in democratic societies.

Civic education helps individuals understand their rights, responsibilities, and role within society. It also encourages meaningful participation in public life and supports democratic institutions.

Through my education and professional experience, I hope to contribute to work that promotes justice, civic participation, and stronger communities.
