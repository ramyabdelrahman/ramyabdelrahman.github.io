Professional Experience

I have professional experience in legal research, legislative reform, policy analysis, civic education, and civil society work. In Egypt, I worked as a lawyer and participated in projects related to constitutional amendments, legislative reform, parliamentary monitoring, and public awareness.

I also served as Executive Director of the Egyptian Democratic Institute from 2009 to 2015. In that role, I worked on civic education initiatives, democratic development projects, voter education campaigns, and reports related to public institutions and legal reform.
